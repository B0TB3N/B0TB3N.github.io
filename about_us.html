Interesting facts about our Organization "Ecocleanup"

Volunteer Army

The organization has a network of more than 50,000 volunteers who are passionate about environmental protection.

Massive trash removal

EcoCleanup has collected and disposed of over 5,000 tons of trash from major cities around the world over the past five years.

Young trailblazers

EcoCleanup's youth groups are particularly active and have launched youth movements for a cleaner environment in many cities.

Celebrity supporters

Celebrities such as Adam Sandler and renowned environmentalist David Attenborough have become ambassadors for EcoCleanup.

Global reach

The organization is active in over 30 countries and is committed to cleaning up major cities around the world.

Education and Awareness

EcoCleanup is actively involved in environmental education and has already visited more than 500 schools to teach children about environmental protection.

Thanks to you, we could go international

We started as a small company headquartered in Zurich, and thanks to our active community, we have been able to create Wastecollection-Centers all over the world.
