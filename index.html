About Us

Together, we keep our Cities clean

In an inspiring display of community spirit, residents from all walks of life are coming together to ensure the cleanliness of our beloved cities. With a shared commitment to environmental stewardship, local initiatives are flourishing, transforming urban spaces into cleaner, greener havens. Together, we are not just keeping our cities clean; we are building a brighter, sustainable future for all.

Together, we help the nature to survive

In a world increasingly threatened by environmental impacts, collaboration is the key to saving nature. Together, we are committed to preserving our natural habitats and biodiversity. Every contribution counts when it comes to helping nature survive.

Recent Events and News

Man collects over 750 Kg of Trash

In Greenville, John Kramer has collected more than 750 kilograms of trash, and his efforts have inspired the community to join in protecting the environment. What started as a solo effort led to a wave of cleanups by schools, businesses and local organizations. John Kramer has become a symbol of the change individuals can make in their communities.

New Trash-Hotspot found near Ohio State University

A new trash hotspot was recently discovered near Ohio State University. This troubling find points to the urgent need to strengthen waste management and environmental protection measures in the region. The university has already announced that it will work with local officials and environmental organizations to combat this undesirable condition.

Thanks to our community, over 10'000 Tree's were planted all over ther World

Thanks to our dedicated community, over 10,000 trees have been planted worldwide. This remarkable success demonstrates the impressive commitment of our members to environmental protection. Together, we are actively working for a greener future.
