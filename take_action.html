About Us

How can you help nature?

You can make a positive impact on nature by reducing your CO2 footprint. Start by using public transportation, biking, or carpooling to reduce emissions from your daily commute. Additionally, adopting sustainable practices like recycling and conserving water can also play a significant role in preserving our environment.

How can we support you in this?

We are committed to supporting your efforts to help the environment. Our platform offers resources and information on eco-friendly practices, making it easier for you to make sustainable choices in your daily life. Feel free to reach out for any specific advice or assistance you may need on your journey towards a greener future.

Our Main-Goals

Keep the Cities clean

In our relentless pursuit of a greener future, our main goal remains crystal clear: to keep our cities clean. Through community-driven initiatives, innovative waste management systems, and eco-conscious urban planning, we are committed to preserving the beauty and sustainability of our urban landscapes for generations to come. Together, we can make our cities shining beacons of cleanliness and environmental stewardship.

Keep the Planet clean

In our relentless pursuit of a cleaner and greener world, our main goals stand as unwavering pillars of commitment. With a shared vision of sustainability, we strive to reduce our carbon footprint, protect natural ecosystems, and promote responsible consumption. Together, we can preserve the beauty of our planet for generations to come.

Restore Nature

Our efforts focus on reforestation, protecting endangered species, and promoting sustainable practices in all aspects of life. Together, we can make a significant contribution to the recovery and conservation of our precious natural resources.

Other ways to help

Use PT in the City, and not a car

In the constant search for environmentally friendly alternatives to driving in the city, public transport is becoming increasingly important. Using PT can not only minimize congestion and parking problems, but also reduce CO2 emissions. A simple switch from car to PT can go a long way toward protecting the environment.

Reduce Food-Waste

Food waste is a pressing environmental problem. Every year, tons of food are thrown away while millions of people go hungry. By shopping more consciously, using leftovers wisely and planning meals, we can help conserve resources and reduce our environmental impact. Reducing food waste is a small step with a big impact on the environment.

Sustainable Shopping

Embrace eco-friendly shopping practices by opting for reusable bags, buying products with minimal packaging, and choosing locally sourced items. Small changes in your shopping habits can make a big difference in reducing waste and supporting local businesses.

"Wie wir Plastik vermeiden: ... und einfach die Welt verändern"

In the inspiring book "Wie wir Plastik vermeiden: ... und einfach die Welt verändern", the author takes us on a transformative journey of how we can drastically reduce our plastic consumption. With practical tips and creative solutions, she shows how each of us can do our part to protect the environment. This book is an indispensable guide for anyone who wants to make a positive impact on our planet.

Documentation • #sustainability #climatechange

"Cowspiracy"

The documentary "Cowspiracy" is a gripping insight into the devastating effects of factory farming on our planet. In impressive images and shocking statistics, the film shows how the meat industry contributes significantly to environmental destruction and climate change. "Cowspiracy" challenges us to rethink our eating habits and explore sustainable alternatives to protect the future of our planet.
